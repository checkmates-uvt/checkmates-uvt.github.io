Tournaments & events

Separate from weekly meetings, we also join and organize tournaments and special events. These range from rapid arenas and open bracket tournaments to lessons and more. Keep an eye on the agenda to find these events and join in!

Open chess

We hold casual chess meetings every friday at 14:45 in the Dante building. See the agenda for the next meeting. Feel free to drop by anytime during these hours for a game or to learn more about chess. All skill levels are welcome!
